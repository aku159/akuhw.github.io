MCU Hardware Logic Simulator

1. Active-Low Input (Internal Pull-up)

A physical switch connected to Ground. The MCU has an internal pull-up to 5V.

Switch: OPEN (Air)
👉
MCU Reads: 1 (5V)

Status: Pin is floating, pull-up brings it to 5V.

2. Active-High Output (Low-Side N-MOSFET)

The MCU controls an N-Channel MOSFET. A High signal (1) turns the 12V Load ON.

MCU Outputs: 0 (0V)
👉
Load: OFF (0V Flow)

Status: Gate is at 0V. MOSFET is open. Load is OFF.
